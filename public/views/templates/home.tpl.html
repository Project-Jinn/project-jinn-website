About Us

We are a small group of developers that live in Lebanon and teach programming. After visting refugee camps we saw stories of people that desperately needed medical help and no way to pay for it. We also saw that many of the talanted devopers that went through are programs went without Jobs due to a lack of tech companies in the Middle East. We decided to build this site to try to help all these amazing individual.
