About Us

We are a small group of developers that live in Lebanon and teach programming. After visting refugee camps we met people that desperately needed medical help and had no way to pay for it. We also saw that many of the talented developers that went through our programs continued without jobs due to a lack of available entry-level tech jobs in Lebanon. We decided to build this site to try to help all of these amazing individuals. V School a company based in Salt Lake City with a campus in Beirut is helping Project Jinn go futher and help more people using there network of teachers and other resources.
